About us

My Online CV is a job management web portal for both users and employers. Users can sign up and create a profile for free. Logged in users are able to view all the jobs posted by employers and can be applied for any number of jobs as per their preference. In addition, users can view the list of applied jobs and can remove them at any time, if they are no longer interested .


Employers can sign up and create their profile, and are allowed to post job openings. Employers are allowed to update or delete their job postings
